# Rental Partner Guide

This guide covers rental partner onboarding, daily operations, and payouts.

## 1. Apply as a Rental Partner

Prepare required identity and business documents, submit your application, and wait for review.

## 2. Set Up Your Shop

- Configure shop name, description, and service area
- Add clear item photos and complete listing details

## 3. Manage Inventory

- Add items, quantities, rates, and variants
- Keep stock updated to avoid failed bookings

## 4. Configure Pricing

Set delivery rates and additional service fees where applicable.

## 5. Manage Bookings

Typical flow:
- Booking received
- Confirm booking
- Deliver items
- Upload delivery proof
- Customer confirms delivery

## 6. Payment Modes

You can require:
- Full payment
- Downpayment plus remaining balance

## 7. Payouts

Payout records move through these stages:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| HELD | Funds are temporarily held |
| READY | Eligible for release |
| PAID | Released |

New rental partners may have a short hold period on early orders.

## 8. Disputes and Compliance

Respond promptly to dispute requests and submit clear evidence.
Repeated policy violations can lead to warnings or suspension.
