# Getting Started

## Sign In

RentalBasic uses Google sign-in.

1. Open the app.
2. Click Sign in with Google.
3. Complete the Google consent screen.
4. Return to RentalBasic and continue.

## Account Roles

| Role | How You Get It | What You Can Do |
|---|---|---|
| Customer | Automatic on first sign-in | Browse vendors, place bookings, manage orders |
| Rental Partner | Apply and pass verification | Manage shop, inventory, bookings, and payouts |
| Admin | Granted by platform owner | Manage platform operations |

## Which App to Use

| App | Who It Is For |
|---|---|
| Customer App | Customers booking rentals |
| Staff App | Rental partners and admins |

## Become a Rental Partner

1. Sign in as a customer.
2. Open Become a Rental Partner.
3. Submit required business and identity details.
4. Wait for review and approval.

## Notifications

Allow browser notifications to receive booking, payment, delivery, and dispute updates.
