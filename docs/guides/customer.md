# Customer Guide

This guide explains the full customer journey from discovery to completion.

## 1. Find Rental Partners and Equipment

- Use the map and search to discover nearby rental providers.
- Open a rental partner storefront to view items, rates, and reviews.

## 2. Place a Booking

Booking has four steps:

1. Select items and quantities.
2. Choose rental dates.
3. Set delivery location.
4. Review total and proceed to payment.

## 3. Pay Securely

Available payment methods include e-wallets and cards.

Rental partners may choose:
- Full payment at checkout
- Downpayment first, with remaining balance before delivery

## 4. Track Booking Status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| UNPAID | Booking created, no payment yet |
| CHECKOUT_PENDING | Payment is being processed |
| DOWNPAYMENT_PAID | First payment complete (downpayment mode) |
| HELD | Payment is secured |
| CONFIRMED | Rental partner accepted the booking |
| IN_DELIVERY | Items are on the way |
| DELIVERED | Rental partner marked delivery complete |
| COMPLETED | You confirmed delivery |
| CANCELLED | Booking was cancelled |
| REFUNDED | Refund was processed |
| DISPUTED | Booking is under dispute review |

## 5. Confirm Delivery

Only confirm delivery after receiving the items in acceptable condition.

## 6. Leave a Review

After completion, submit a rating and optional comment.

## 7. Cancel and Get Refunds

Cancellation refund depends on timing before the rental start:

| Timing | Refund |
|---|---|
| Before rental partner confirmation | Full refund |
| More than 48 hours before start | Full refund |
| 24 to 48 hours before start | Partial refund |
| Less than 24 hours before start | No refund |

## 8. Raise a Dispute

If there is a serious issue, open a dispute from your booking details and provide evidence when requested.

## 9. Chat and Notifications

Use in-app chat for coordination. Enable notifications for real-time updates.
